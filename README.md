**Introduction of the Project**

**Project Title: Hospital Management System**

**Introduction:**
Hospitals play a vital role in life and health of person, from mild infections to severe injuries a person relies on the hospital. Good medical care relies on good record keeping. Hospital's management must keep check on doctors, staff, patients and departments etc. to work better and smoothly. In a world where manual record keeping is challenging our system will overcome this challenge.
The hospital management system is an innovative designed to revolutionize healthcare administration. Its purpose is to enhance the efficiency of managing doctors, patients, and departments within a hospital. By automating tasks such as medical records management and staff coordination, the system aims to eliminate paperwork, reduce errors, and improve overall patient care. 
                 
**Problem Statement:**
 Traditional hospital management involves manual record-keeping, leading to inefficiencies, errors, and delays in accessing critical information. There is a need for a system that can automate processes, reduce paperwork, and improve overall hospital management.
Proposed Solution: 
Our proposed solution is a Hospital Management System utilizing linked lists to manage doctors, staff, patients, and departments efficiently. This system will provide a centralized platform for record-keeping, appointment scheduling, and resource management, ultimately improving the overall workflow of the hospital.

**Scope of the Project:**
The scope of this project includes the implementation of modules for managing doctors, patients, and departments. It encompasses functionalities such as appointment scheduling, medical records management, and staff coordination. The system aims to enhance communication among hospital staff, reduce administrative overhead, and improve the overall quality of patient care.

**Modules:**
1.	Manage doctor
2.	Manage patient
3.	Manage department
4.	Manage Staff
5.	Manage Inventory
6.	Manage Bill

**Modules Description:**

1. Manage Doctor:
   - Create: Add new doctors to the system with relevant information.
   - Read: Display a list of all doctors with their details.
   - Update: Modify information of existing doctors.
   - Delete: Remove a doctor from the system.

2. Manage Patient:
   - Create: Register new patients with necessary details.
   - Read: Display a list of all patients with their information.
   - Update: Modify information of existing patients.
   - Delete: Remove a patient from the system.

3. Manage Department:
   - Create: Add new departments to the hospital.
   - Read: Display a list of all departments and their details.
   - Update: Modify information of existing departments.
   - Delete: Remove a department from the system.


4. Manage Staff:
   - Create: Add new staff to the system with relevant information.
   - Read: Display a list of all staff members.
   - Update: Modify information of existing staff members.
   - Delete: Remove a staff member from the system.
5. Manage Inventory:
   - Create: Add new inventory to the system with relevant information.
   - Read: Display a list of all inventory with their details.
   - Update: Modify information of existing inventory.
   - Delete: Remove inventory things from the system.

6. Manage Bills:
   - Create: Add new bill to the system with relevant information.
   - Read: Display a list of all bills with their details.
   - Update: Modify information of existing bill.
   - Delete: Remove bill things from the system.
